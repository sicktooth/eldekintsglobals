Who We Are

ELDEKINTS GLOBAL SERVICE LTD

ELDEKINTS GLOBAL SERVICE LTD is an indigenous service provider to the oil and gas industry in Nigeria. It was incorporated as a limited liability company in Nigeria in 18 of march 2018 to carry on the business of drilling support for the oil and gas industry; environmental management; marine logistics and support; and project management. It is also involved in the business of marketing, supply and trading of crude oil and petroleum products.
