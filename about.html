Who We Are

ELDEKINTS GLOBAL SERVICE LTD

ELDEKINTS GLOBAL SERVICE LTD is an indigenous service provider to the oil and gas industry in Nigeria. It was incorporated as a limited liability company in Nigeria in 18 of march 2018 to carry on the business of drilling support for the oil and gas industry; environmental management; marine logistics and support; and project management. It is also involved in the business of marketing, supply and trading of crude oil and petroleum products.

Our Mission

Our mission is to recruit, retain, and reward a high-performance workforce while delivering excellent and innovative services to our clients. We are committed to building capacity within the communities where we operate, ensuring sustainable development, economic empowerment, and responsible corporate citizenship.

Our Vision

Our vision is to achieve a sustainable competitive advantage as a leading indigenous oil and gas service provider through strong corporate governance, effective risk management, operational excellence, and strict adherence to environmental preservation and safety standards.
