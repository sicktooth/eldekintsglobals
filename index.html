You've Come To The Right Place

ELDEKINTS GLOBAL SERVICE LTD is an indigenous service provider to the oil and gas industry in Nigeria. It was incorporated as a limited liability company in Nigeria in 18 of march 2018 to carry on the business of drilling support for the oil and gas industry; environmental management; marine logistics and support; and project management. It is also involved in the business of marketing, supply and trading of crude oil, petroleum products and production of chemical products. Our mission is to strengthen our client base by ensuring excellence and innovation in service delivery.
